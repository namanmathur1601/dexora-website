How Dexora works

Dexora turns inputs into controlled execution using a Planner→Executor model, governed tools, and tenant-isolated knowledge bases.

Planner decides steps

Workflow logic is deterministic. The planner proposes steps; the orchestrator enforces allowed actions and guardrails.

Specialist agents execute

Document, SOP, validation, integration, optimization, and explainability agents work together — with retries and timeouts.

Actions are safe

Validation, approvals (HITL), idempotency, and audit logs prevent “AI did something weird” incidents.

Outputs are structured

Not just text. Dexora returns tables, cards, and downloadable artifacts (PDF/Excel) — ready for operations.

Core capabilities
